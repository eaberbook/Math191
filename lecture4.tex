\section{Bias-Variance Decomposition}

If we let $z$ be a random variable, then we can express the variance of $z$ as follows.

\begin{align}
Var(z) &= E[(z-E(z))^2]\\
&= E[z^2 - 2zE(z) + (E(z))^2]\\
&= E(z^2) - 2E(zE(z)) + E[E(z)^2]\\
&= E(z^2) - 2(E(z))^2 + (E(z))^2\\
&= E(z^2) - (E(z))^2
\end{align}
